Which Printers?

Q. Which printers does cups know about and what’s their stati?

A. lpstat knows:

$ lpstat -v
device for HP-Color-LaserJet-CP3525: socket://MA-PRINT6-1.pc.fracsec.com:9100
$ lpstat -a
HP-Color-LaserJet-CP3525 accepting requests since Wed 12 Mar 2014 03:09:15 PM EDT

Note that I’m not nearly l33t enough to setup or configure printers on the command line. No, I had to use

$ system-config-printer

and step through the GUI. Someday, I’ll figure out how to do it with nothing but bash and cups utilities.
